📖 Lý thuyết: Past Continuous

A · Công thức: was/were + V-ing

I / he / she / it → was doing · we / you / they → were doing
Phủ định: wasn't / weren't + V-ing · Nghi vấn: Was/Were + S + V-ing?

B · Khi nào dùng?

This time last year I was living in Hong Kong. (đang xảy ra vào thời điểm đó)
What were you doing at 10 o'clock last night?

C · So sánh với Past Simple

Past Continuous (đang diễn ra)

We were walking home when I met Dan. (đang đi giữa chừng)
Kate was watching TV when we arrived.

Past Simple (hoàn thành)

We walked home after the party. (đi hết cả đoạn đường)
Kate watched TV a lot when she was ill.

D · Một việc xảy ra giữa chừng việc khác

Matt phoned while we were having dinner. (Continuous = nền; Simple = sự kiện)
I hurt my back while I was working in the garden.
⚠️ Stative verbs không dùng Continuous: We knew each other well. (not were knowing)
