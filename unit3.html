📖 Lý thuyết: Continuous vs Simple (so sánh)

Present Continuous (I am doing)

Hành động đang xảy ra lúc nói, hoặc trong giai đoạn này:

The water is boiling. Be careful.
She's learning Italian. (chưa xong)
I'm living with friends until I find a place. (tạm thời)
You're working hard today.

Present Simple (I do)

Thói quen, sự thật, điều lâu dài:

Water boils at 100°C. (luôn đúng)
Most people learn to swim as children. (nói chung)
My parents live in London. (lâu dài)
What do you usually do at weekends?

⚠️ I always do vs I'm always doing

I always go to work by car. (thói quen bình thường)
I'm always losing my keys. (xảy ra quá nhiều, thường mang nghĩa phàn nàn)
Paul is always complaining. (= complains too much)
