📖 Lý thuyết: Stative verbs – động từ không dùng Continuous

Động từ KHÔNG dùng Present Continuous (stative verbs)

likewantneedprefer knowrealiseunderstandrecognise believesupposeremembermean belongfitcontainconsistseem
I'm hungry. I want something to eat. (not "I'm wanting")
Do you understand what I mean? (not "Are you understanding")

⚠️ think — 2 nghĩa khác nhau

I think Mary is Canadian. (= believe/opinion → Simple)
I'm thinking about what happened. (= consider/contemplating → Continuous OK)
Nicky is thinking of giving up her job. (= she is considering it → Continuous OK)

see / hear / smell / taste — giác quan

Do you see that man? (not "Are you seeing")
The room smells. (not "is smelling")
You look well today. or You're looking well today. (look/feel — cả 2 đều OK)

is/are being — hành vi nhất thời

He is being selfish. (= đang hành xử ích kỷ lúc này, không phải bản chất)
He is very selfish. (= bản chất, nói chung)
⚠️ Sam is ill. (not "is being ill") — không dùng "being" với trạng thái thụ động
